Title: Musst man Tieren essen?

----

Year: 2013

----

Text: Vegetarian was yesterday, today vegan is all the rage. On this tour, you can eat crepes, burgers and pizzas made without a trace of cow's milk, cheese, eggs or meat. You'll also find out where to buy tofu sausages, spelt-almond milk or quinoa, so you too can cook vegetarian/vegan at home.
